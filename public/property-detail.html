Description

Welcome to "EasyG" where easy living meets the heart of Greenbrier. Low-maintenance comfort with space to relax and enjoy the day. Picture starting your morning with coffee on the covered front porch before heading out for a walk past the neighborhood pond and fountain. Inside, the well-maintained single-level layout offers vaulted ceilings, wide-plank LVP flooring, and a cozy gas fireplace that anchors the main living space. The kitchen flows easily into the dining and living areas and includes a bright breakfast area, currently arranged as a sitting and workspace, offering flexibility for hobbies, reading, or morning coffee. The primary suite features a tray ceiling and abundant natural light. Additional highlights include a two-car attached garage and corner location. Community amenities include clubhouse, pool, fitness center, and maintained grounds. At EasyG, everyday living really can feel this simple.
